Magic Month Calculator

What is a "magic month"? On a normal month you are paid either two times a month if paid biweekly, or four times if paid weekly. Throughout the year though there are several months that produce one more paycheck than normal.

Instructions: Enter a date that you are paid. Select either Weekly or Biweekly. THe magic months that are presented are based on the year of the date entered.
